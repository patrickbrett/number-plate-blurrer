Why blur?

[AFTER]

When you upload a photo of your car online - to social media, to a blog, or to a sales website - criminals may try to forge your number plate so that fines are directed to you instead of them.

Blurring your number plate is the easiest way to avoid this.

Upload a photo to Number Plate Blurrer and it automatically finds where any number plates are, then blurs them. You can fine-tune the position and then download the image with all the plates blurred out.

Your photos are used to help our algorithm learn how to find number plates, so it’s constantly improving. This is the exact same as many of the big car sales portals - except all the code for our system is completely open source, so you can be sure everything is kept safe and sound.
